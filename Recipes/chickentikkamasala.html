Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is prepared by marinating boneless chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, and a blend of spices, including turmeric, garam masala, coriander, cumin, and chili powder. The marinated chicken is then skewered and grilled or broiled until cooked through, imparting a smoky flavor. Separately, a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce is prepared by sautéing onions, garlic, and ginger, then adding tomatoes, cream, and additional spices. The grilled chicken is then simmered in the sauce, allowing the flavors to meld. The dish is typically garnished with fresh cilantro and served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Naan or rice (for serving)
