Potato Curry

Description

Potato curry, a flavorful and hearty dish, begins with peeling and cubing potatoes. The preparation starts by heating oil in a pan and adding cumin seeds, allowing them to sizzle and release their aroma. Finely chopped onions are then saut√©ed until golden brown, followed by the addition of minced garlic and ginger. Spices such as turmeric, coriander, cumin, and chili powder are added and toasted briefly to enhance their flavors. Tomatoes, either chopped or pureed, are added next, cooking until they soften and blend with the spices to form a rich base. The cubed potatoes are then added to the mixture, coating them well with the spice blend. Water or broth is poured in to cover the potatoes, and the curry is simmered until the potatoes are tender and the sauce has thickened. The dish is often garnished with fresh cilantro and served hot with rice or bread.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or mustard oil)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped or pureed
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)
